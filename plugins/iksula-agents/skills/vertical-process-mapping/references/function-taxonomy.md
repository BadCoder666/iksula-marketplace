# Standard Function Taxonomy (completeness checklist)

Use this as the checklist so no function is forgotten. Adapt names to the vertical (e.g. a marketplace adds Seller Ops; a D2C brand adds Performance Marketing; a 3PL emphasises Transportation & Network Design). Keep the four groups so files stay comparable. The "easily-forgotten" functions marked ⚠ are the ones most often skipped — always include them.

## Commercial / Revenue
- Field Sales (beat/route, order booking, market intel, door collections)
- Inside Sales / Telesales
- Key Account Management (EDI/punchout, JBPs, QBRs)
- Sales Operations (targets, incentives, beat design, analytics)
- Marketing / Trade Marketing (incl. ⚠ MDF / co-marketing claims)
- Category & Assortment (range planning, principal/brand mgmt, NPD)
- Pricing & Margin Management (list/tier pricing, deal desk, ⚠ margin-leakage)
- B2B / D2C Ecommerce Channel (portal, search, promotions, adoption)
- Customer Service / Order Management

## Supply Chain / Operations
- Procurement / Purchasing (Procure-to-Pay)
- Demand & Inventory Planning (forecast, replenish, ⚠ near-expiry/dead stock)
- Warehouse / DC Operations
- Logistics / Transportation (incl. ⚠ freight-invoice audit, POD)
- ⚠ Returns / Reverse Logistics (incl. return-to-principal claims)
- ⚠ Quality & Regulatory Compliance (licensing, batch/recall, cold-chain, audit)

## Finance
- Order-to-Cash (AR, Credit, Collections, cash application, deductions)
- Reconciliation & Claims (bank/vendor/customer recon, ⚠ scheme claims, ⚠ ITC)
- Accounts Payable
- General Ledger & Close
- ⚠ Tax & Statutory Compliance (GST returns, e-invoice/e-way, TDS, notices)
- FP&A / Business Finance (profitability, MIS, scenarios)
- ⚠ Treasury (cash forecasting, working capital, ⚠ bank-charge audit, financing)

## Enabling / Support
- ⚠ Master Data Management (+ Catalog/Content): item/customer/vendor/price master, dedup, ⚠ HSN/tax classification, governance
- IT / ERP / Integrations (incl. EDI onboarding)
- Data / Analytics / BI
- ⚠ HR / People (high-volume field & warehouse workforce)
- ⚠ Legal / Contracts (principal agreements, obligation tracking)
- Admin / Facilities

## The recurring high-value patterns to probe in every vertical
1. **Unstructured document understanding** — orders, remittances, bank statements, scheme circulars, price lists, ledgers, GRN, POD, freight bills, CoA, invoices, complaint photos, contracts.
2. **Master-data spine** — dirty master data is why most automation historically failed; fix first.
3. **Recovery & leakage** — scheme claims, MDF claims, return-to-principal credits, freight/bank overcharges, ITC, early-pay discounts, margin leakage, AR deductions. Usually scattered with no owner.
