# prospect-outreach-research — Deliverable templates

## SINGLE — Prospect dossier (`.docx`, Iksula brand)
1. **Header** — prospect name · title · company · date · "Pre-engagement / cold".
2. **Snapshot** — what the company does, nature of business (vertical label), key categories, public/
   private, estimated revenue (range + source).
3. **What's happening** — news in the last 12 months (dated, sourced); any trigger to act now.
4. **Website & stack** — eCommerce platform (signal used), PIM (or "unknown", confidence), approx.
   product count (how derived).
5. **The prospect** — role & responsibilities; online presence (LinkedIn/X/Substack/YouTube, notable
   posts); **direct connect** (degree + shared connections).
6. **Where Iksula fits** — named `solutions-catalogue` offers matched to the need; content to forward.
7. **Outreach** — the 5 messages.
8. **Confidence & open questions** — what's confirmed vs inferred vs not found; flag if it warrants
   `client-research`.

## SINGLE — One-page brief (`.docx`/`.md`, 1 page)
Who they are (2 lines) · the hook / why now · fit angle (named offer) · direct-connect path · the 3
talking points · the 5 messages inline.

## SINGLE — Outreach messages (copy-ready block)
```
Connect note 1 (≤300 chars): ...
Connect note 2 (≤300 chars): ...
Warm InMail 1: ...
Warm InMail 2: ...
Direct InMail: ...
```
